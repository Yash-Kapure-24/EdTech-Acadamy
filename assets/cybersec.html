The Complete Cyber Security Course : Hackers Exposed!

This course presents the fundamental concepts and best practices of cybersecurity. Topics include network security, cryptography, risk management, security policies, access control, incident response, and ethical hacking. Students will gain hands-on experience with security tools and techniques to protect computer systems and networks against cyber threats. The course emphasizes a holistic, risk-based approach to developing a robust cybersecurity strategy.
